# Overview of Vatican II

The **Second Vatican Council** (Vatican II) was the twenty-first General, or Ecumenical, Council of the Catholic Church. It was a movement for the renewal of the Catholic faith for a new era. The Council, in which 2,500 Bishops took part, was held in four sessions between 1961 and 1965 and it produced [[vaticanII-documents|sixteen documents]].
